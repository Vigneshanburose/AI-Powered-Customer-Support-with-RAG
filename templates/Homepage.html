Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI-powered system work?

Our system uses Twilio to handle multi-channel communication and employs advanced AI models like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to retrieve the most accurate, up-to-date information from your knowledge base. It can respond to customers through voice, text, WhatsApp, and more.

Can I integrate the AI chatbot into my existing website?

Yes! Once you upload your knowledge base, we instantly create a chatbot that you can embed into your website with a few lines of code.

What communication channels are supported?

The system supports voice calls, SMS, WhatsApp, and website chat. Customers can choose the platform they’re most comfortable with to reach your business.

How long does it take to set up?

After uploading your knowledge base, you’ll have a fully functioning AI chatbot in just minutes. Embedding it into your site is a quick and easy process.

Can I provide phone support through the system?

Absolutely! After setting up, you will get a dedicated phone number that customers can call. The AI answers their questions, eliminating the need for live agents.
